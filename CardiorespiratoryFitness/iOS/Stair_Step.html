About Stair Step test

Why this test

Recording your heart rate recovery after going up and down a stair step for 3 minutes has been used to assess ones cardiovascular fitness level.

What to do

  1. Find a stair step that you are comfortable going up and down for 3 minutes (can be inside or outside).
  2. Measure your heart rate within this app before the 3 minute stair step session.
  3. Step up and then down a single step at a speed of 24 steps per minute for 3 minutes.
  4. Measure your heart rate immediately after the 3 minute stair step session.
